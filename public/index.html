About Me

I'm a 16-year-old researcher and student at Aqbobek International School in Aktobe, Kazakhstan, with a passion for cybersecurity, competitive programming, and robotics.

On January 17, 2026, I was accepted as an NSRI Ambassador. Just six weeks later, on March 2, 2026, I was promoted to the position of NSRI Director for my school. In this role, I'm building a strong research presence and helping fellow students discover the transformative power of structured research.

My mission: to democratize access to world-class research mentorship and help high school students publish their work in major journals and competitions.
